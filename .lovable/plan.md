

# Games and Connect — Events, Games & Travel Platform

A vibrant, youthful community website for hosting outdoor games, parties, trivia nights, and travel experiences in Ghana.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation & Core Pages

### Design System & Layout
- **Energetic Color Palette**: Red, Yellow, Blue, Green team colors with a dark/light neutral base
- **Typography**: Bold, modern headlines with clean body text
- **Reusable Components**: Navbar, Footer, Page Headers, Cards, Buttons
- **Animations**: Smooth fade-ins, hover effects, and subtle scaling

### Home Page
- Hero section with rotating event images and tagline: *"Vibes • Games • Travel • Community"*
- Upcoming Events slider showcasing featured events
- Quick action buttons: Join Community, Register, View Gallery
- Testimonials carousel from past attendees
- Newsletter subscription section

### About Page
- Brand story and mission statement
- History timeline of Games and Connect
- Meet the Team grid with photos and roles

---

## Phase 2: Events & Activities

### Events Page
- Grid of upcoming event cards (image, date, location, price)
- Filter by event type
- **Event Detail Page**: Full description, image gallery, countdown timer, registration form

### Game Day Page (Nexus 9 Partnership)
- Banner explaining the monthly partnership
- Schedule display: Every first Saturday
- Four team showcase: Red, Yellow, Blue, Green with colors
- Past Game Day photo gallery
- Registration form

### Trivia Friday Page
- WhatsApp trivia explanation
- Next session date and theme display
- Past winners spotlight
- Registration form

## Phase 3: Travel & Community

### Travel Page
- Upcoming trips showcase (Cape Coast, Akosombo, etc.)
- Trip cards with itinerary previews
- Pricing breakdown and installment plan info
- Booking form

### Join Community Page
- WhatsApp community join button
- Volunteer program signup form
- Loyalty program teaser (coming soon)

## Phase 4: Gallery & Contact
